About Me

Siqi Lai earned his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University in 2022, where he was mentored by Prof. Chenliang Li. He is currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the Artificial Intelligence Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Prof. Hao Liu. His research focuses on large language models (LLMs), data mining, and intelligent transportation systems. Siqi has published in leading conferences such as SIGIR and SIGKDD and has served as a Program Committee (PC) member for SIGKDD.
